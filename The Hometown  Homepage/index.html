Visit Kasansay

Enjoy culture and history in this vibrant district near the mountains.

Top three activities to do at Kasansay

Enjoy the masterpieces of ornamental art

The Aloxon Jome Mosque is an excellent place to understand 20th-century decorative art in Central Asia.

Go for a picnic in a local park

The Ulug` Chinor park is perfect for picnics, healing in nature or just chilling out.

Discover the history of the city

Visit the maqbara Maxtumi A`zam and feel the spirit of history come alive.
